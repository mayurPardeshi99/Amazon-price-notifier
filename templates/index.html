Do you shop online ?
And want to save money while shopping ?

Can I Buy is a free price drop alert that helps you to save money while shopping online on amazon india.
You can add multiple products and manage them and we will notify you as soon as the price is dropped.

Advanced

You can add products to your wishlist through your dashboard and manage them. You can add as many products as you want and it's free.

Fast

As soon as your wishlist's products price will drop we will notify you on your email.

Daily Price Update

Your wishlist product's price will update everyday and you can see it on your dashboard.
